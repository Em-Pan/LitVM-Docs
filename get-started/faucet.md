## Faucet

The LitVM Testnet Faucet provides free testnet zkLTC for development and testing.

**URL:** [https://liteforge.hub.caldera.xyz](https://liteforge.hub.caldera.xyz)

### How to Get Testnet zkLTC

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Visit the Faucet

Go to [https://liteforge.hub.caldera.xyz](https://liteforge.hub.caldera.xyz)
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Connect Your Wallet

Click **"Connect Wallet"** and select your preferred wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, etc.)
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Request zkLTC

* Enter your wallet address (if not auto-filled)
* Click **"Request"** or **"Claim"**
* Wait for the transaction to process (~30 seconds)
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Verify Balance

Check your wallet balance — you should see zkLTC available for gas fees
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

### Faucet Limits

* **Amount per request:** Small amount sufficient for gas fees
* **Frequency:** Limited to prevent abuse
* **Network:** LitVM LiteForge Testnet (Chain ID: 4441)

### Alternative: Bridge from Testnet LTC

If you need more zkLTC or the faucet is unavailable:

1. Acquire testnet LTC from a [Litecoin testnet faucet](https://testnet-faucet.com/ltc-testnet/)
2. Use the [LitVM Bridge](https://liteforge.hub.caldera.xyz) to convert LTC → zkLTC
3. zkLTC will appear in your wallet on LitVM
