## Community Guidelines

{% hint style="info" %}
We're building an inclusive, respectful community. Please:

Do:

* Be respectful and constructive
* Help newcomers
* Share knowledge
* Report scams and phishing

Don't:

* Spam or shill
* Harass others
* Share financial advice
* Impersonate team members
{% endhint %}

***

{% hint style="warning" %}
Scam Warning ⚠️

LitVM team will NEVER:

* DM you first asking for funds
* Ask for your private keys
* Require you to "verify" your wallet
* Send you to unofficial websites

Always verify:

* Official domain: litvm.com
* Official Twitter: @LitecoinVM

Report scams to our community moderators.
{% endhint %}
